## What is APVA?

The APVA framework is the industry-leading Enterprise SaaS platform for tracking, evaluating, and maximizing the **True Value Yield (TVY)** of Generative AI applications.

While other observability tools tell you *how fast* your AI generated tokens, APVA tells you **how much money those tokens saved your company.**

### The Enterprise Moat

APVA is built for Fortune 500 scale, featuring:

1. **Proprietary SLM RAG Evaluation**: We use specialized ONNX-quantized Small Language Models to evaluate RAG faithfulness 100x cheaper than OpenAI.
2. **Global Edge Ingestion**: Our SDKs fire telemetry payloads directly into a globally distributed Cloudflare Worker network, buffering into Apache Kafka to protect your core databases.
3. **Sub-Second OLAP Analytics**: Built on top of ClickHouse, APVA aggregates millions of True Value Yield rows in under a second for real-time Executive Dashboards.
4. **Enterprise Identity**: Native SAML/OIDC Single Sign-On (SSO) integration for Okta and AzureAD.
